*DATA VISUALIZATION. noun. [da-t? vi-zh?-w?-l?-za-sh?n] : Connection of a dataset to a visual interface in order to facilitate the exploration and understanding of information. Playful interactivity lies at the heart of the process and is based on the assumption that it greatly improves memorization.

Synonym: dataviz, exciting display of content. Antonym: Spreadsheet, rigid databases, boredom.

EVENTS

We provide assistance at any stage of the data visualization process: consultancy, conception of innovative information products, data design, creation of visualisation, and training to data journalism.

We equally work with media companies, agencies, corporate communication services, web marketing specialists, administrations and artists.

We tell
stories
from data

We aim to create genuine information experiences around data visualization. As a matter of fact, we believe that traditional, rigid charts and graphs are no longer suited to explain the complexity of information. Likewise, obscure documents and never-ending spreadsheets are little incentive to go deeper in the story behind the data. At Inceptum, we rather want to create interfaces calling for interaction and exploration based on a playful user experience.

We gather a wide range of skills, from information architecture, design, statistics to web development and media strategy. Our workflow is focused on user experience, as we create pathways to information that generate interest and increase memorization.

Concert venues in Paris:

Geographical proximity, musical similarity?

This visualisation displays the main concert venues in Paris, and the similarity of their main musical genre. The wider the link between two venues, the greater the musicalsimilarity.

The size of the dots indicates the seats capacity of the venue.

The data has been extracted from music website Last.fm and is automatically updated on a weekly basis.
